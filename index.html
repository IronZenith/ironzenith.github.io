Leadership Structure

When planning our FC, we wanted to ensure from the start that we had a leadership system that would be readily adaptible to the needs of the Free Company, and always allow for fresh ideas and new input. At the same time, we know that there needs to be a stable core to maintain the guiding principles of the organization and ensure we stay true to our goals. As a result we settled on having a two-house system of leadership: a permanent Council and a temporary elected team of Officers.

Our current leadership team consists of 3 Council Members and 0 Officers. For more information about how our leadership works, check out the static charter. You can also find a full list of our officers and council members further down!

Rules

Below is the most basic outline of our expectations for members and officers alike to follow.

  • First and foremost be an adult. If you are under 18 and we find out, you're gone. No exception.
  • Be respectul of your Free Company fellows.
  • Do not use the Free Company for personal gain.
    • No begging for gil, resources, etc.
    • Not using the FC to advertise your own ventures.
    • Be respectul of public FC chest resources.
  • No topics that will actively cause arguments (religion, politics, etc.)
  • Keep spoilers out of public spaces.

In addition, where reasonable, we expect all members to abide by the FFXIV ToS regarding in-game topics. Being an 18+ Free Company we will not be actioning on ToS violations ourselves, but we want to remind all members to be mindful of these policies. If you are looking to appeal an action against you, learn more about our escalation policies, or are just in general intersted in a full rundown of our policies, expectations, etc., click here to view our full FC charter.

Events

Fridays mean Fire! Join us every Friday for events, contests and more!

Phoenix's Favor - Monthly Lottery

The last Friday of every month is Lotto time! Throw your gil into the pyre and take a chance to rise from the ashes with newfound wealth and riches!

How to Enter

  • Tickets are 25,000 gil each. A random number between 1-999 should be submitted with each ticket. Each number must be unique.
  • A player can enter up to 4 times. (100,000 gil maximum)
  • Players can enter anytime up through the last Wednesday of the month, 11:59PM ET.
  • A list of participants will be posted to the discord the following day. You can confirm your entries until 11:59PM ET Thursday.

How to Win

  • A number will be randomly drawn ingame in the FC House at 11:00PM ET Friday. Closest number without going over wins the pot!
  • If multiple indviduals had the same winning number, the prize will be distributed evenly amongst all winners.
  • If the winning number is exact, a bonus prize (announced immediately prior to the drawing) will also be awarded to the winner(s)!
  • Winner(s) will be posted in the discord following the ingame announcement. You will have one week to claim your winnings.
  • In the event that no one claims winnings, the lottery will be rerolled.
  • In the event that more than one person wins but not all winnings are claimed, the remainder will be distributed to those who have claimed.

Fire's Fortune - Weekly Treasure Maps

Join us every Friday at 9:00PM ET in the FC House yard for Treasure Maps!

How to Enter

  • Meet in the park below the FC house prior to 9:00 PM EST.
  • Set your Search Info icon to "Looking for Party."
  • Bring your own maps! Some people may have extra maps to share, but the FC is not officially providing maps for runs.

Map Rules

  • Listen up! Read chat close and follow your party leader's instructions! We give them outlines for making our map runs go smoothly.
  • Share the loot! Our FC map runs are free-for-all. Don't be greedy with your maps!
  • Don't delay! Once the parties have left the yard, it is up to the leader whether they will be inviting more people on or not. If you're late, you might miss the fun!
  • Follow the leader! We follow a set path through the zones based on their MSQ appearance. For Endwalker, this means we travel this path:
    Thavnair > Garlemald > Mare Lamentorum > Elpis > Labyrinthos > Ultima Thule
